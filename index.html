Hard Rock the way you Remember

Are you tired of pop music?

Bored going to live shows just to hear 1 or 2 songs as rocking and extreme as you remember?

Ready and waiting for something more?

Born with hard rock surging through their veins, Dying Breed never skipped a beat. Dying Breed brings a diverse family of talented musicians and passionate fans together for a live experience like none other that will leave everyone begging fors more.

The artists you love

Covering all your favorites from Heart, Stevie Nicks/Fleetwood Mac, Dio, Black Sabbath, Def Leppard, Scorpions, Judas Priest, Metallica, Iron Maiden, Queensryche, Boston, Journey, Dokken, and many more with a constantly evolving setlist.

The intensity you need

The members of Dying Breed bring an energetic, in-your-face sound you haven't heard since Reagan was in office. Combining over a century of experience in hard rock entertainment recognized by the Maryland Hall of Fame, Dying Breed works to capture the emotion and power of music from decades past.

Experience the magic with the last of a Dying Breed.
